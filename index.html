Privacy Policy for AppliancePal

Last updated: June 24, 2026

AppliancePal is a single-developer iOS app built by HzTech. Your privacy matters, and the design of this app reflects that.

What we collect

Nothing. AppliancePal does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on any server. There are no accounts, no logins, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party trackers.

Where your data lives

All appliances, maintenance logs, custom cycles, and settings you enter are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Your data never leaves your iPhone or iPad. If you delete the app, the data is removed with it.

Notifications

The app schedules local maintenance reminders using iOS notifications. These are processed entirely on-device by Apple's notification system. We do not send push notifications from any server.

In-app purchases

The optional one-time unlock is processed by Apple via StoreKit. Apple's own privacy policy governs that transaction. We do not see, store, or receive your payment information.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
